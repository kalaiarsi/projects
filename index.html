Vehicle Faremeter

Designed a hardware prototype (Version 1 using Arduino and Version 2 using Rasperry Pi B) of a vehicle faremeter. Features include:

  • Talks to a centralized server through GPRS (for regular fare updates-Over The Air)
  • GPS for positioning
  • Appropriate display
  • Thermal printer to print bill
  • Connectivity with vehicle's physical odometer to analyse tampering
  • PANIC button for emergency

Battery Diagnostics

Developed hardware as part of Proof of Concept. Was used to find the health status of Li-ion batteries and not just the charge left. The idea was to use chirp signal (frequency domain analysis) to test battery health and predict battery failures.
